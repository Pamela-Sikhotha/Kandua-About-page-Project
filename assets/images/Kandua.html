About Kandua
 
 

Kandua is a fast-growing, mission-driven tech startup.

We build easy tools that empower people professionally. Tools that shorten the distance between having a skill and making a living from it. Our flagship product, Kandua.com, is South Africa’s largest online home services marketplace: A seamlessly designed platform, that connects great home service providers to the people who need them, powered by pioneering technology and a passionate, talented team. At Kandua, being excellent at what we do matters. That’s how we make an impact. That’s how we grow as people and as a business. That’s how we’ve made over R180 million worth of work opportunities happen, and we’re just getting started.
